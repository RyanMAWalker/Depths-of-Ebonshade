Overview

The Depths of Ebonshade is a fan made horror inspired Dungeons and Dragons adventure. The campaign is designed to be a short campaign with party sizes of 3-5 members, we recommend you create new characters or bring a long ones you are not afraid to lose for it is likely to be their last adventure.

DM Difficulty: Medium

Party Difficulty: Hard

Levels: 1-10

Story

Months ago a maroon mist descended upon the town of Ebonshade, engulfing the area of which in inhabitants have yet to be seen.

Your party currently resides in the town of Narrowmarsh a large inland trading community bolster by the network of cannals that run through it. As you gather in the adventures guild looking to take on your next job the doors to the guild edge open as the damp putrid stench of rotting fruit invades the room. Your party turns to see a robed skeletal figure entering moving towards you.
